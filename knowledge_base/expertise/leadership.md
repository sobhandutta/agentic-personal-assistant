# Leadership and Team Building — Sobhan Dutta

## Leadership Philosophy
Sobhan Dutta is a seasoned people leader who has built and scaled UX/UI teams from zero at multiple startups. He believes that great teams are the product of intentional hiring, continuous mentorship, and a culture of psychological safety where designers and engineers can experiment and learn.

## Team Building Experience
- Built the UX & UI team at Atayalan Inc from scratch as the founding U.S. hire
- Built and scaled the UX & UI team at Elisity Inc as Head of UX & UI Engineering
- Led globally distributed teams at Nuance (Microsoft) across multiple time zones
- Hired, mentored, and grew high-performing designers and engineers across all roles

## Startup Experience
Sobhan has been a founding member at multiple startups, giving him deep experience in:
- Building functions from zero without established processes or tools
- Making high-impact decisions with limited resources
- Moving with speed while maintaining quality
- Fundraising support through product excellence (contributed to Elisity's $26M raise)
- Establishing culture, process, and tooling from the ground up

## Cross-Functional Alignment
A recurring theme in Sobhan's career is his ability to align design, product, and engineering teams. He views cross-functional friction as one of the largest sources of waste in software development. His approach includes:
- Embedding designers within engineering squads (not separate design teams)
- Shared OKRs between design and engineering
- Design system adoption as a shared responsibility
- Regular design reviews that include engineering stakeholders

At Ataya, this approach increased release velocity by 50%.

## Managing Distributed Teams
Sobhan has extensive experience leading globally distributed teams:
- Led offshore development and design teams at Nuance across multiple time zones
- Managed globally distributed UX & UI teams at Elisity
- Maintains delivery velocity and quality standards regardless of team geography
- Established communication rhythms and processes for async collaboration

## Strategic Leadership vs Hands-On Execution
Sobhan is comfortable operating at both strategic and execution levels:
- At Ataya: 70% strategic, 30% hands-on
- At Elisity: 60% functional leadership, 40% hands-on
- At Nuance: Principal-level technical leadership with deep individual contribution

This flexibility makes him effective both as a director-level leader and as a hands-on technical contributor when the situation demands it.

## Mentorship and Growth
Sobhan believes in developing talent within his teams. He invests in:
- Regular 1:1s focused on career growth, not just task status
- Creating stretch opportunities for high-potential team members
- Building a culture of constructive design critique
- Supporting team members in developing both design and technical skills
