# Head of UX & UI Engineering — Elisity Inc

## Role Overview
Sobhan Dutta served as Head of UX & UI Engineering at Elisity Inc in San Jose, CA from February 2020 to January 2022. He led UX and UI engineering for Elisity's cloud-native Zero Trust network security platform, with a 60% functional leadership and 40% hands-on responsibility split.

Elisity is a cybersecurity company building a Zero Trust network security platform for enterprise customers. Sobhan partnered with the Chief Product Officer to shape product strategy, architect the frontend layer, and lead a globally distributed team.

## Key Responsibilities
- Setting UX vision and technical frontend architecture for a complex enterprise security product
- Building and leading a globally distributed UX & UI team
- Owning end-to-end UX research and design operations
- Partnering with the CPO on product strategy and roadmap
- Ensuring seamless design-to-code handoff and design system adoption
- Embedding usability testing and user research into the product development cycle

## Major Achievements at Elisity
- Built and led a high-performing UX & UI team that delivered the company's first MVP within 3 months
- End-to-end UX research and design operations led to a 30% improvement in task success rates and strong customer satisfaction scores in enterprise pilots
- Drove product-led growth that contributed directly to Elisity securing $26 million in Series A funding within the first year
- Drove 30% feature adoption by embedding telemetry, UX research, and usability testing into the product cycle
- Reduced frontend rework by 40% by ensuring seamless handoff between design and code through design system adoption
- Shaped UX vision and technical architecture resulting in 100% on-time delivery across all major product milestones

## Product Context
Zero Trust network security is a complex domain requiring the UX to make highly technical concepts (identity-based micro-segmentation, policy enforcement, network visualization) accessible to enterprise IT administrators. Sobhan led the challenge of translating this technical complexity into intuitive, enterprise-grade interfaces.

## Technologies and Tools
Figma, React.js, Angular, JavaScript, CSS3, design systems, telemetry platforms, user research tooling, Agile/Scrum methodologies.
